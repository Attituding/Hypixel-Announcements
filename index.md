# Hypixel News
Hypixel News is a project that sends you the latest news from the Hypixel Network. As soon as new posts by the Hypixel Admins are found on the forums, you will receive a neat message in your chosen Discord channel.

To get started, take a look at the announcement categories available on the next page. Then, you can start getting news by choosing to either use the Discord Bot or manually follow the news channels from the [support server](https://discord.gg/d2kKwqcUsv).
